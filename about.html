Research Background

School-Based Science Mentorship Programme (2015)

My first research experience was in 2015 when I took part in the School-Based Science Mentorship Programme. I conducted research work every week as a part of a team in my school’s research laboratory. Together with my team, I wrote a report and created a poster for this project. We were awarded gold for our exemplary performance.

Chemistry Research (2016-2017)

I conducted a research in chemistry in a team of 3. This was a self-proposed research. We investigated the effects of semi-permanent and permanent hair dye on the strength of Asian hair over a span of 7 weeks under the school’s individual studies program. This project was selected for individual research studies (IRS) presentation organised by the Gifted Education Branch.

Biology Extended Essay (2018-2019)

I conducted an individual research in biology as part of the extended essay for the International Baccalaurate Diploma Programme.

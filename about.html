Research Background

In this section, I detail research projects that I have been involved in for more than a year.

-----------

(1) Undergraduate Research Experience on CAmpus (URECA), August 2021-June 2022

Project Title: SCSE21018 - Vulnerability Analysis of Microprocessors - I

Project Description: Currently, I am taking part in URECA to conduct research on the vulnerabilities in Intel SGX. Commercial processors such as Intel processors have multiple security protocols such as secure boot processes, memory encryption, remote attestation, and address space layout randomization. In my research, I am focusing on the attacks that lead to the remote attestation protocol being compromised.

Supervisor: A/P Anupam Chattopadhyay, Co-PI & Thrust (iv) Lead

(2) Biology Extended Essay, 2018-2019

I conducted an individual research in biology as part of the extended essay for the International Baccalaurate Diploma Programme. I investigated the anti-cancer and anti-genotoxic properties of different concentrations of Azadirachta indica leaf extract by using Allium Cepa Root Tip Assay, followed by statistical analysis using ANOVA and Tukey Testing.

DSTA Brainhack: Today I Learn AI Hackathon 2021

In June 2021, I took part in a hackathon hosted by the Defence Science and Technology Agency (DSTA), Singapore. We were provided tutorial videos and codes to learn computer vision and sound processing using Pytorch. Having no prior knowledge, I had to learn the concepts within two weeks. Then, I had to complete two challenges with my team (consisting of 5 members, including me) before the 2-day hackathon. During the 2-day hackathon, my team and I had to solve four more challenges.

Shopee Product and Design December 2020

In December 2021, I took part in a Product and Design Competition in a pair. Our idea was to allow users to store their body measurements in the Shopee App itself and have sizing recommendations based on the user's measurements. Users can save up to three measurements (e.g. their own, their children's, their spouse's measurements) to help order on behalf of others. We came up with the user story, the user requirements and the design mockup for our idea.

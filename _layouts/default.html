About Me

Passionate About Data Science and Analytics

I am Samiksha. I am currently a Year 2 Student studying a double degree in Computer Science and Economics at Nanyang Technological University(Singapore).

I am a person who loves to push boundaries and take on challenges. As such, I decided to take on a double degree. I enjoy working with numbers and large amounts of data. I am always curious to analyse such data and derive new information from it. Besides working with numbers, I take joy in solving logic problems and using programming to solve such problems.

This portfolio features the projects I have done. The articles provide an overview of what the project is and what I have learnt from it. While doing these projects, I have picked up many skills.

This website was built in May 2021. Posts here are updated accordingly. This website is built using HTML, CSS, Jekyll and Github Pages.
